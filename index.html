#DJStroud Bio

#DJStroud is Josh Stroud, a #1 EDM DJ from Los Angeles and San Francisco, California. #DJStroud has played to sold out shows at venues like the Graham-Graham in SF, the Whiskey A Got'Ems Club, and Kap Slap at UC Berkeley. Josh spins EDM, house, trance, bass, hip-hop, and EDM pop hits. Josh is inspired by artists like Kaskade, Armin Van Buuren, and Carl Cox to create a unique and danceable mix powered by bass, beats, and melodic vocals.

Josh can be found in San Francisco and Los Angeles hanging out with his secret admirer :) and mixing up DJ sets on YouTube, Spotify, and his podcast "#DJStroud In The Mix" launching September 2025. Find him on Spotify and Apple Music soon :)
